Listrunner SECURITY BASICS

  • Your patient data is safe

    We use better security than the banks. All patient health information is strongly encrypted, both at rest and transit using certified regulatory compliant servers.

  • Control access

    You decide who has access to your list and for how long. Easily add and remove rotating staff and learners. With audited records, you always know who has seen what.

  • Cloud protection

    Listrunner allows you to enable a cloud mode so no data is stored locally on your device. If your phone is lost or stolen, no personal health information is left on the device.

  • Easily Managed Permissions

    We only access your data through our controlled servers, protected by industry-standard security measures both physical and electronic, and never import or store your database.
